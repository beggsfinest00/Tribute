The Story of Death

Death is one of the 4 Horsemen of The Apocalypse. He is the 2nd horseman introduced in the Darksiders franchise.

He is the leader of the bunch, but fury wants to take over the roll of leader.

Death must fight the minions of "The Tormentor" which are called Abominations.

As stated before "The Tormentor" is the main enemy of death.
